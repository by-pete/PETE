# ref-content — Content & Thought Leadership Workflows

## LinkedIn Thought Leadership Pipeline
Robert positions as: AI strategy advisor who bridges manufacturing operations and emerging AI capabilities.

### Post Types (rotate weekly)
1. **Insight post**: Specific AI application in manufacturing with business outcome
2. **Framework post**: Mental model for C-suite AI decision-making
3. **Case study post**: Anonymized client result (with permission)
4. **Contrarian post**: Challenge conventional wisdom about AI in manufacturing

### Post Standards
- Hook in first line — specific, surprising, or challenging
- 150-300 words max
- End with clear takeaway or question
- No generic AI hype — always grounded in manufacturing reality
- Voice: Direct, experienced, slightly provocative

## Knowledge Base Ingestion (Berman Model)
When Robert drops a URL or reference:
1. Fetch full content (article, video transcript, PDF)
2. Extract key insights relevant to manufacturing AI, operations, consulting
3. Tag by category: [TECH] [STRATEGY] [MARKET] [METHODOLOGY] [CLIENT-RELEVANT]
4. Store in memory with metadata (source, date, relevance score)
5. Confirm: title, type, key takeaway, chunk count

## Content Research Workflow
When preparing content or client materials:
1. Query knowledge base for relevant saved content
2. Search web for recent developments
3. Synthesize into Robert's voice and strategic framing
4. Flag any IP boundary considerations

## Video/Presentation Pipeline
For speaking opportunities or client presentations:
1. Research topic depth
2. Create outline with hook, key points, supporting data
3. Draft speaker notes in Robert's voice
4. Suggest visuals/frameworks that reinforce the message

# ref-bunting — Bunting Magnetics Operations Reference

## Company Overview
- $50M revenue manufacturing company, Newton, Kansas
- Products: Magnetic separation equipment, metal detection systems, tramp metal extraction
- ERP: Epicor
- Robert's built systems: BuntingGPT (customer-facing), production analytics, operational intelligence platforms

## Key Systems
- **BuntingGPT**: Customer-facing AI assistant for product inquiries, specs, applications
- **Production tracking**: Real-time dashboards monitoring schedule adherence, quality metrics
- **Quality management**: Automated flagging of quality events with impact calculation
- **Data pipelines**: Extract from Epicor ERP, transform, load into analytics

## Reporting Structure
- Robert → Seth (direct report)
- Mission-critical systems require Seth's awareness for major changes

## Product Knowledge
- Magnetic separation: plate magnets, grate magnets, magnetic pulleys, eddy current separators
- Metal detection: industrial metal detectors for food, plastics, packaging, mining
- Applications: food processing, recycling, mining, plastics, pharmaceuticals, aggregates

## Operational Metrics to Monitor
- Production schedule adherence (target: >95%)
- Quality hold frequency and duration
- Capacity utilization by work center
- On-time delivery rate
- Customer complaint rate and resolution time

## Communication Style for Bunting Context
- Professional but concise with leadership
- Tie everything to business outcomes (revenue impact, customer satisfaction, operational efficiency)
- Use manufacturing terminology naturally (cycle time, throughput, yield, OEE)
